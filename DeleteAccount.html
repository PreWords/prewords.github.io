Account Deletion — PreWords

If you wish to delete your PreWords account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below. We take your privacy seriously and will ensure all your personal information is permanently removed from our systems.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please send an email to:

fezelx.studios@hotmail.com

Important Requirements:

  • You must send the email from the same email address that is associated with your PreWords account
  • Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line
  • Clearly state in the email that you want your account and all associated data to be deleted

⚠️ Important Notice

Data Deletion Process:

  • All your personal data and account information will be permanently deleted within 24 hours of receiving your request
  • This action is irreversible - once deleted, your data cannot be recovered
  • You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion process is complete
  • After deletion, you will no longer be able to access any PreWords services with this account

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon processing your deletion request, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account profile and personal information
  • All app usage data and preferences
  • Any stored content or user-generated data
  • Purchase history and transaction records
  • All communications and support tickets

Contact Information

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please contact us at:

fezelx.studios@hotmail.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 48 hours.
